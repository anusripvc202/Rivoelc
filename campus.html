OUR CAMPUS & FACILITIES

At Rivo, we believe that the environment is the third teacher. Our campus is a sanctuary of learning, designed to inspire curiosity and nurture the well-being of every child.

Spacious Campus

Our 5,500 square feet campus offers ample room for learning, play, and exploration. Each classroom is well-planned with excellent ventilation and natural light, ensuring a fresh and healthy atmosphere for children.

Hygiene & Cleanliness

We maintain uncompromising standards of hygiene. From regularly sanitized classrooms to clean washrooms and play zones, every corner of RIVO is dedicated to the safety and health of our little explorers.

Safety & Supervision

Peace of mind is paramount. Our campus features comprehensive CCTV surveillance and a student-teacher ratio of 1:10, ensuring that every child is seen, heard, and supported in their learning journey.

Our Learning Philosophy

We create meaningful learning experiences where children grow through creativity, curiosity, exploration, and joyful discovery every day.
